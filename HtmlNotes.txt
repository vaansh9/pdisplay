TO CHANGE FONT ATTRIBUTES
1. font color="red"
2. face="Arial"
3. size="16pt"
4. bgcolor="blue" (to change background color)

TO KEEP TEXT MOVING IN SOME DIRECTION
1. marquee 
2. marquee behavior="slide"
3. marquee behavior="alternate"

TYPES OF HEADING
1. <h1>-<h6> (font size decreases)

ORDERED LIST
1. <ol type="1">
2. <ol type="a">
3. <li> (to add elements in list)
 
UNORDERED LIST
1. <ul type="boxed">
2. <ul type="disc">
3. <ul type="circle">

FOR CREATING A TABLE
1. <style>
		thead{background-color:black;color:white}
		tbody{background-color:black;color:white}
		tfoot{background-color:black;color:white}
    </style>

2. <table border=1> to adjust thickness of the border of the table
    <align="center"> for  alignment of the table
    <bgcolor="lightblue"> background color of the table
    <cellspacing=10>
    <cellpadding=10>
